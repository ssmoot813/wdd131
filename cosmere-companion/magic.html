Understanding Cosmere Magic

One of the most distinctive aspects of the Cosmere is that each world has its own unique magic system. These systems follow consistent underlying rules, but they feel very different in how they are used and experienced.

Some systems are structured and rule-based, while others are more mysterious or tied to the environment of their world. This variety is part of what makes the Cosmere so engaging for readers.
